Info

    Chris Norris is a graphic designer and strategist with 7+ years experience working as a creative partner across brand, publication, and digital design projects. With a focus on the intersection of technology, art and design his work includes non-profit, mission-driven, community organizations and academic institutions in addition to collaborations with various design agencies and studios.

    From 2015 to present, he has worked as a co-founder to shape Alder Studio, a practice that supports this ongoing design work. Alder works with businesses and organizations to produce and manage design projects, events and social activations to create a more vibrant future.

    Recently, he is working alongside Jonathan Harris and Jasmine Parsia to shape the design circle within Mosaic, a technology focused construction startup.

    He lives and works in Burlington, Vermont.
